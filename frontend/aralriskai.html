Data Sources

  • Sentinel-2 surface reflectance for NDVI, NDSI, and salinity indices
  • Sentinel-5P aerosol indicators for dust exposure context
  • MODIS AOD and land-cover products for atmospheric and surface context
  • ERA5-Land climate variables for temperature, precipitation, wind, and soil moisture

Model

  • Tree-based regression model for next-month degradation risk score
  • Classification layer for low, medium, and high risk classes
  • Time-aware evaluation with 2019-2023 training data and 2024 validation data

Output

  • Risk score scaled from 0 to 1 for selected locations
  • Risk class for interpretation and communication
  • Feature importance and environmental indicator trend summaries
